About Me


I worked as a barista for about a year and a half while in college. It helped me develop my communication skills, multitasking, and efficiency during stressful, time-sensitive situations. That, and, of course, a crippling addiction to coffee.
Hello World! I am Kylie Kosek and I go by Lee. I graduated the University of Michigan in December 2024 with a major in computer science and a minor in art & design. I build accessible, responsive websites and software using modern technologies like React.js, JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS. My projects showcase my experience in front-end development, full-stack development and UI/UX design.
